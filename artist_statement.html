Artist Statement

My artworks reflect who I am as an IT student and as a person. In this collection, I explored different ways of expressing my ideas—from drawing what I see to creating abstract designs and digital artworks. Each module helped me understand how to use elements like line, color, and shape to communicate something meaningful.

Most of my works are inspired by my daily life, my experiences as a student, and my interest in technology. I also included Filipino-inspired designs to connect my work to my culture. At the same time, I used modern themes like AI and programming to show how technology is a big part of my life today.

Through my artworks, especially my photo narratives, infographic, and memes, I shared both serious and fun sides of my journey. Some show my struggles, while others show humor and creativity. Overall, my visual world is a mix of art and technology—it represents how I think, what I experience, and how I express myself in a digital generation. .

Jhester M. Pascual — 2026
